Vendor Maintenance & Listings

Change Vendor Default G/L Account#

 Last Revised: 07/31/13

Change Vendor Default G/L Account# is used to edit the recommended/default General Ledger account number that is listed for Accounts Payable options.

When selecting an existing General Ledger Account, you may either enter the G/L Account# or any word(s) in the description. If this company/organization has multiple locations and you have defined a segment of the G/L Account# as the location, then when maintaining the G/L account for automated G/L postings that should vary by location, enter Ls in the location segment of the G/L account number.

For example, if the Sales account is 4000 and the second segment of the G/L account# designates the location, then specify 4000-LL if you have a two digit location, 4000-LLL for a three digit location, etc.
