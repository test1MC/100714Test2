Vendor Purchases Report

 Last Revised: 1/03/14

Vendor Purchases Report summarizes purchase history by vendor groups.  

You have two sequence options:  

1. to list vendors alphabetically (based on their alphasort),

OR

2. to rank vendors according to their amount of purchases, highest to lowest.   Purchase amounts are rounded to the nearest dollar.

This report allows for several format options.   Vendors can be grouped by Type or according to a Master Vendor Number.  

In order to group vendors by Type, you must first define codes for Vendor Types in Vendor Type Maintenance on the VML menu. Once these codes are defined, you may assign a Vendor Type Code to each vendor through Vendor Maintenance.  

Examples of Vendor Type Codes might be:

  • F = Freight
  • I = Inventory
  • O = Other

Vendors can also be grouped by a common Vendor Master Number that is entered on the vendor record through Vendor Maintenance.   For example, if you wish to group together all vendors having a number in the 100s, you can assign each of them a vendor master number of 101.
