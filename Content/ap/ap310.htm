Accounts payable Reports

A/P Cash Requirements Report

 Last Revised: 01/10/14

The Cash Requirements Report is used to plan for future cash needs, thus maximizing cash flow efficiency.  

Cash requirements are broken down by vendor for 4 consecutive periods. The operator specifies whether or not the report will assume that discounts are taken.

Report detail includes vendor number/name, invoice number, invoice date, cash requirements for operator-specified due dates, and total cash requirements for each due date. Open invoices put on hold are combined with future cash requirements.
