Accounts payable payment Processing

Manual Payment Register

 Last Revised: 02/04/14

This Register is an audit report summarizing all manual payment entries.   A Register must be printed before a manual payment batch can be updated.   Upon selecting a batch, you will enter a General Ledger posting date.

 

Information is categorized by type of entry: Checks Printed, Checks Reversed, Other Manual Payments (such as Automated Withdrawals), Voided Checks.

Each type of entry is further itemized by check date, payment method established for check, bank account, check number, vendor, payment method that was established during invoice entry, invoice number, invoice date, invoice amount, discount applied, and payment amount.
