Accounts Payable

A/P Payment Search

 Last Revised: 03/11/14

This feature allows you to display payment information for processed payments.  It is a helpful tool for locating  payments that may have been incorrectly entered.

Dynamo will search for invoices by four different criteria:

  • Check Number#
  • Payment Date
  • Payment Amount
  • Bank Code

 

 

 

If multiple payments match the criteria entered, they will be listed by date.  Information displayed: The Vendor Number and Name, the bank, Check Number, Check Date, Gross Amount, Discount Amount, Net and the Batch Number the payment was processed in.
