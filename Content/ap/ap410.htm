A/P Invoice Search

 Last Revised: 01/10/14

This feature allows you to search for invoices even if you do not know who the vendor is.  This is a useful function in cases where a payment may have been posted to the wrong vendor.

Dynamo will search for invoices by four different criteria:

  • Invoice Number (if known)
  • Purchase Order Number
  • Invoice Amount
  • Invoice Note

Enter any of the criteria you remember and Dynamo will search for it.

 

If multiple invoices match the criteria entered, they will be listed by date.  Information displayed: The Vendor Number and Name, Invoice Number, Invoice Date, Invoice Amount and the Batch Number it was processed in.

Upon choosing an invoice:

 

From this screen, you may proceed to Vendor Inquiry for further details on the invoice; for example, if and when it was paid.
