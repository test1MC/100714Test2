Accounts payable Reports

A/P On Hold Listing

 Last Revised: 01/10/14

A/P On Hold Listing displays invoices that have been put On Hold during Invoice Data Entry.  

This report lists invoices by vendor and is similar to the Trial Balance Report but will also include the number of days the invoice is past due (based on the current date).

The listing can be printed for all vendors or an individual vendor.

If locations are applicable to your system, you will be given the option to display On Hold listings for each location, for an operator-specified location, or for all locations.
