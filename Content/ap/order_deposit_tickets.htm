Guidelines for Ordering Dynamo Deposit Tickets

 Last Revised: 04/03/14

Please provide the following information to your forms provider when ordering Dynamo Deposit Tickets:   

The deposit ticket is printed on 8 1/2 x 11 paper. The paper should be perforated 3 1/2 inches down from the top.

The top portion of the deposit ticket should have the following pre-printed information:

  • the phrase "deposit ticket" on the top
  • Company name and address
  • "Date:" two inches down from the top and one inch from the left
  • your bank information
  • your account and routing numbers above the perforation
  • 1 inch down from the top and 5 inches in from the left should have the following:
    • CASH:
    • CHECKS:
    • OTHER:
    • TOTAL ITEMS:
    • TOTAL DEPOSIT:

 

The bottom portion of the deposit ticket should say "DEPOSIT DETAIL" in the middle just below the perforation.
