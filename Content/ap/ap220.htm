Accounts payable payment Processing

Check Register

 Last Revised: 02/04/14

The Check Register is a list of all payments made through the Check Printing program, including printed checks and payments made through Electronic Fund Transfer or Automated Withdrawal.  

Note: It does not include payments made through Manual Payment Entry. Manual payments are listed on the Manual Payment Register.  


The payment batch number and general ledger posting date prints at the top. Itemized information on the report includes:

  • Check Number
    This column remains blank if the Payment Method was coded as T for Electronic Funds Transfer or W for Automatic Withdrawal because a printed check was not issued.   The 'P' column will show these codes.
  • Check Date
    This is the date that printed on the checks.
  • Vendor Number and Name
    One-time vendors will have a single-character vendor number, such as M, followed by the Payee's name.
  • P Column
    Payment Method Code:
    • blank = normal
    • S = separate check
    • T = funds transfer
    • W = automated withdrawal)
  • Invoice Number, Date, and Amount
  • Discount Amount (if applicable)
  • Net
  • The check amount

The Amount/Discount/Payment columns will display a subtotal for all blank check numbers; that is, those payments that were made by Automated Withdrawal or Electronic Funds Transfer.   This subtotal is included in the grand total of each column.

A printed copy of the Check Register must be produced before the check information can be updated to the permanent files.
