Bank Balance Inquiry

 Last Revised: 06/26/13

Bank Balance Inquiry displays bank balances.

Hitting "Enter" on this page will pop up a grid displaying the description, balance, pending, and net:

The pending column shows the amount of funds that have not been reconciled in Bank Balance Reconciliation.

To advance the grid to the right, hit the Arrow Right key, where the reconciled funds, date created, G/L#, and Account# are displayed:
