Accounts payable Reports

Display A/P Cash Requirements

 Last Revised: 01/22/14

This display is a valuable tool for those customers who choose to keep their checking account balance at the minimum amount necessary to cover payables.  

It is used to predict future cash needs based on the dates indicated by the operator.  

The default dates consist of the current date and the next 9 consecutive weeks; however, these dates can be overridden.

Cash requirements for all open invoices displays, including those on hold.   The display is divided into two categories: Cash Requirements if Discounts are Taken and Cash Requirements if Discounts are Not Taken.
