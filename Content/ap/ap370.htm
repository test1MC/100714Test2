Accounts payable Utility Functions

Invoice Batch Control Check

 Last Revised: 09/20/13

This report is used to confirm the integrity of the Accounts Payable Batch Control File. It shows whether or not the control totals equal the sum of all invoices (the number and dollar value). The count variance and the total variance should equal zero. If the power went out, an accidental reboot occurred, or an application was unexpectedly closed, a variance may appear in one of those variance columns. The report contains:

  • the batch number
  • the S
  • the Open Date
  • the Date Updated
  • the General Ledger posting date
  • the count
  • the total
  • the account count
  • the account total
  • the count variance
  • the total variance
