A/P Historical Invoice Register

 Last Revised: 01/13/14

The A/P Historical Invoice Register provides a complete recap of invoice activity that affects the Accounts Payable balance within the operator-specified time frame.   Invoices entered in the system after the operator-specified date are excluded.  

This report details the increases (credits) to Accounts Payable that took place during this period.   Invoices are listed alphabetically by vendor.

The Historical Invoice Register can be used to help reconcile the General Ledger A/P Totals to the to the Accounts Payable detail.

 

Detailed information includes:

  • Vendor Number and Name
  • Batch Type and Batch Number
  • Invoice Number
  • Invoice Date and Due Date
  • Days (If a balance is due, this column displays a positive or negative number. Based on the current date, a positive number indicates the number of days until the invoice is due. A negative number indicates how many days ago the invoice was due.)
  • Discount Due Date
  • Hold Status (If the invoice is on hold, a Y displays in this column.)
  • Invoice Amount
  • Discount Amount
  • Balance Due

Individual Batch Totals (for both A/P and M/P batches) are summarized at the bottom of this report.   The report also provides a summary of debit and credit postings to the General Ledger expense accounts that took place as a result of invoice entries.
