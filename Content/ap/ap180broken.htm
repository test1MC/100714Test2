Accounts payable payment Processing

Payment Selection

 Last Revised: 01/31/14

Payment Selection scans all unpaid invoices and automatically selects invoices for payment that meet the selection criteria you've entered in the fields listed below.  

Note: once an invoice has been selected for payment, its status will display as "Pending" in Vendor Inquiry - A/P Invoice option.

 

The first step is to start a new payment batch or select an existing batch for payment. (This batch number refers to a payment batch and is not related to the invoice batch number.) If a new batch is started, the next unused batch number is automatically assigned.

Invoices will be selected for payment based on the following entries:

  • Selected Vendor(s):   To make a payment to a SINGLE vendor, enter the vendor number, alpha sort name, phone #, or zip.   To scan unpaid invoices for ALL vendors, press the spacebar.

  • Payment Cycle Code:   Payments may be scheduled using payment cycle codes.   This allows you to pay a group of vendors coded by number. Vendors can be assigned a payment cycle code through Vendor Maintenance - Accounts Payable. If you do not use payment cycles, press Enter to bypass.

  • Select Invoices Due On or Before: This defaults to today's date, but it can be overridden to any date within the next 30 days.   Any invoices with a due date on or before this date will be selected for this payment batch.
  • Select Invoices with a Discount Due On or After: The default date is 15 days prior to the current date but can be overridden. Any invoices with a discount due date on or after this date will be selected for this payment batch.
  • Select Invoices with a Discount Due On or Before: The default date falls 10 days after the current date but can be overridden.   Any discounts with a discount due on or before the default date will be selected for this payment batch.

The ability to override the default discount dates gives you the option of taking those discounts whose due dates have already passed.    

The invoices that meet the selection criteria are marked for payment.   As invoices are being selected for payment, the screen will display a cumulative total for the following:   

  • Number of Checks:
  • Total Payment:

If the information displayed is acceptable, return to the main menu and print the Payment Selection Register.

If the information displayed is not acceptable, you have the following options:

  • Clear all payments selected automatically; proceed with next step.
  • Revise the criteria for payment you previously entered. The cumulative totals will be adjusted to reflect the number of invoices added or deleted.

- or -

  • Proceed to Payment Selection Maintenance to add invoices to this payment batch, delete invoices from the batch, make a partial payment on one or more invoices, or to release an invoice for payment that was put on Hold.
