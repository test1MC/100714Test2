Customer Listings, Labels, Export

 Last Revised: 07/31/13

Customer Listings, Labels, Export is used to display a list of operator-designated customer ranges for marketing or contact purposes.

Formats
Listings

Use this option to list all of the customers the operator specified.

Labels

Use this option to print labels to be pressed onto mail (exclude or include customer names).

Export Use this option to export a spreadsheet of customers into other programs.
Note: to print labels, you need 8.5" x 11" paper with three labels across and ten labels down.
