Customer Listings

 Last Revised: 01/14/14

This application is used to create a listing of a specified group of customers entered into Dynamo.

The operator must select the sequence and the range. To continue, the operator must select the format:

Options
1. Pricing Fields

Selecting this option will display customers with price# information.

2. Customers with Price Date

Selecting this option will display customers with special pricing.

3. Tax Exempt Customers

Selecting this option will display the contact information of customers who are tax exempt.

4. Customers with Salesperson Not-on-File

Selecting this option will display contact information of customers with no salesperson listed.

5. Customers by Terms Code

When selecting this option, the operator must choose to:

  1. include all,
  2. exclude customers with Standard Company Terms, OR
  3. exclude customers with Standard Company Terms and terms codes 90-99

The listing will show the terms code, description, customer number and name, and the last sale date.

6. Inactive Customers

When selecting this option, the operator must choose a last sale date.

The listing will show:

  • the customer number and name
  • the city
  • the state
  • the last sale date

7. Customers with Alternative Invoice Delivery

Selecting this option will display:

  • the customer number and name
  • the ID#
  • the contact name
  • the fax number
  • the e-mail address

Where applicable:

  • the invoice delivery method (E=Email, F=Fax, A=ANSI EDI)
  • the EDI receiving group the customer is part of (Grp)
  • the electronic data interchange identifier number (EDI)
