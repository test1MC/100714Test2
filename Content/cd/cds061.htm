Message Processing

 Last Revised: 02/11/06

This program is also available by typing the letter M at any Dynamo menu or using the M Hotkey. Message processing is used to send a multi-line message to one or more other Dynamo operators. The messages are delivered immediately and the recipient is informed that a message is waiting.

Memo Writing

Memos are like messages except they are printed or faxed instead of delivered to another Dynamo operator. Contact Excellware to configure your sent messages to include your company logo on any faxed memos.  Memo writing interfaces with customer and vendor contacts and fax numbers.
