Printer & Fax Management

Email Log

 Last Revised: 09/19/08

Dynamo applications can send internet email.  This application lists recent email history.

The most recent emails are listed first.

You can highlight an email and touch enter to display additional information.

The 'Email Type' field is "H" for HTML email, and blank for regular text.

After touching F4 to the initial view that includes all emails, you are prompted to re-display, change record selection criteria, or F4 to return to the menu.

The change record selection option allows you to specify a single login ID to filter the display list to the emails sent by that operator.

This log file is purged using 'Purge Fax & Email Logs' on the PFM menu.
