Dynastore Reports

 Last Revised: 05/14/14

This application allows a company to view all reports in its Dynastore storage.

 

When you open this application, choose your reports based on:

  1. who they were created by,
  2. the application(s),
  3. the date range,
  4. any comments, and
  5. the status.

After choosing these items, a list of reports will display on your screen. You can choose to view or delete a record. If you choose to view it, it will open in a PDF file on your computer.
