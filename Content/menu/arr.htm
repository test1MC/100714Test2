Accounts Receivable Reports

Menu Overview

 Last Revised: 02/19/15

The Accounts Receivable Reports menu allows the operator to view reports related to accounts receivable, such as finance charge listings, ageing details, trial balances, and statements. The operator can also compute finance charges from this menu in addition to running the A/R Month End Update.

  1. Compute Finance Charges
  2. Finance Charge Listing
  3. Finance Charge Maintenance
  4. Finance Charge Update
  5. A/R Ageing - Detail
  6. A/R Ageing - Summary
  7. A/R Ageing - by Transaction Salesman
  8. A/R Trial Balance
  9. A/R Listing by Invoice Number
  10. A/R Statement Printing
  11. A/R Month End Update
