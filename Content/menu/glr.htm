General ledger Reports

GLR Menu Overview

 Last Revised: 02/18/15

The General Ledger Reports Menu contains several reports and financial statements. These include reports showing G/L Transactions, posting details, inventory valuations, un-vouchered receipts, special order inventory, inventory in transit, inventory used in house, and trial balances.

  1. G/L Transaction Report
  2. G/L Posting Detail for IC Transactions
  3. Payments on Open Sales Orders
  4. Daily Inventory Value
  5. Unvouchered Receipts Listing
  6. Special Order Inventory Detail
  7. Inventory In Transit
  8. Inventory Used in House
  9. Sales by G/L Account
  10. G/L Trial Balance
  11. Standard Financial Statements
