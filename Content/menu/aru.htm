Accounts Receivable Utility Functions

Menu Overview

 Last Revised: 02/19/15

The ARU menu contains options for maintaining cash drawers and payment methods. It also allows the operator to view A/R Ageing, audit & reset customer control totals, and set A/R parameters.

  1. Cash Drawer Maintenance
  2. Cash Drawer Paid Out Code Maintenance
  3. Payment Method Maintenance
  4. Display Accounts Receivable Ageing
  5. Audit Customer A/R Control Totals
  6. Reset Customer A/R Control Totals
  7. A/R Parameter Maintenance
  8. A/R Parameter Maintenance (Old)
  9. A/R Transaction Maintenance
