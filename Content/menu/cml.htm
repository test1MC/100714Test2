Customer Maintenance & Listings

Menu Overview

 Last Revised: 02/18/15

The programs on this menu are used to manage customer account information, to classify customer categories, to maintain salesperson account information, to create customer and salesperson listings, to create customer mailing labels, and to export customer contact information. The Customer Maintenance Utilities menu can be reached from this menu as well.

  1. Customer Maintenance
  2. Salesman Maintenance
  3. Customer Type Maintenance
  4. Customer Selection Code Maintenance
  5. Customer Listings
  6. Customer Listings, Labels, Export
  7. Customer Label Printing
  8. Export Customer Email Addresses
  9. Salesman Listing
  10. Customer Maintenance Utilities (CMU)
