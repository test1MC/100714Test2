File Maintenance & Listings

Menu Overview

 Last Revised: 02/18/15

This menu links to several other menus that are used for File Maintenance. File Maintenance enables operators to enter new records, search for and modify existing records, and in some cases, delete records that are no longer needed.

  1. Item Maintenance & Listings (IML)
  2. Customer Maintenance & Listings (CML)
  3. Prospect Maintenance & Listings (PML)
  4. Vendor Maintenance & Listings (VML)
  5. Cost & Price Maintenance (CPM)
  6. Label Printing Functions (LAB)
  7. Company Wide Parameters (CWP)
  8. Dynaweb Management Software (WMS)
