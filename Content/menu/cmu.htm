Customer Maintenance Utility Functions

Menu Overview

 Last Revised: 02/18/15

The programs on this menu are used to determine unused customer numbers, manage customer numbers, verify and correct customer addresses, print customer addresses, and purge customer addresses by last-used date.

  1. Unused Customer Number Listing
  2. Change Customer# Entry
  3. Change Customer# Listing
  4. Change Customer# Update
  5. Customer Address Correction
  6. Ship to Address Listing/Purge
