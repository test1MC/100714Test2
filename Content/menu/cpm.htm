Cost & Price Maintenance & Listings

Menu Overview

 Last Revised: 02/18/15

This menu is used to manage and list pricing details such as vendor costs, formula codes, and selling prices. Vendor pricing can be entered manually or imported from a file provided by the vendor. The Flexible Price List can be used to create a customized price list containing only information specified by the operator.
