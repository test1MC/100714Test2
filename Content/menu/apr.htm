Accounts payable Reports

Menu Overview

 Last Revised: 02/18/15

This menu provides a number of optional Accounts Payable reports. Ageing and cash requirements reports are useful for managing payments. Historical reports list details about past invoices and payments. The Vendor Purchases Report lists details of how much has been paid to a particular vendor for the current and prior 12-month periods. Various Form 1099s can also be printed from this menu.
