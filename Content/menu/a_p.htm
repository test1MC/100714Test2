Accounts Payable

A/P Menu Overview

 Last Revised: 02/19/15

This menu provides the application to post vendor invoices to Dynamo Accounts Payable. Invoice Data Entry is used when a vendor sends an invoice, and Recurring Invoice Maintenance and Generation are used when a vendor does not send invoices for recurring charges. This menu also includes A/P Invoice Maintenance where information can be corrected on updated transactions. There are also powerful search options for finding invoices or payments. From this menu, the operator can also access the Accounts Payable Payment Processing menu, the Accounts Payable Reports menu, and the Accounts Payable Utility Functions menu.
