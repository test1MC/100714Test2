General ledger

G/L Menu Overview

 Last Revised: 02/18/15

This menu is used to define your General Ledger accounts and to process General Ledger transactions. Dynamo is fully integrated which means that most transactions post directly to the General Ledger, reducing manual effort required to produce timely and accurate postings.

  1. G/L Account Maintenance
  2. Chart of Accounts Listing
  3. G/L Inquiry
  4. G/L Posting Entry & Maintenance
  5. G/L Posting Report
  6. General Ledger Reports (GLR)
  7. General Ledger Utility Functions (GLU)
