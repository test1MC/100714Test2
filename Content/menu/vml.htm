Vendor Maintenance

VML Menu Overview

 Last Revised: 02/13/15

This menu is used to manage vendor and buyer information through inquiry and maintenance. Other applications include vendor label printing, a method for correcting vendor addresses, and a method for changing default vendor general ledger accounts.
