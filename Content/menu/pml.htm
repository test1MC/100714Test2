Prospect Maintenance & Listings

Menu Overview

 Last Revised: 02/13/15

This menu is used to manage information about potential customers. Dynamo provides a mechanism to manually enter prospects or import prospect lists from other sources. Other applications to help salespersons manage contact lists and a notepad for recording future marketing details are located on this menu.
