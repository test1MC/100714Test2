Inventory Transaction Listing

 Last Revised: 02/06/14

The Inventory Transaction Listing application prints lists of all transactions created in Inventory Transaction Entry. The listing will show the transaction date, the warehouse, the transaction type, the item number and description, the login ID of the person who completed the transaction, whether or not it is not on file, the quantity, the cost, the G/L amount, the G/L Debit, the G/L credit, and the G/L Sub Type. The information contained on this report has been entered into the system but has not been posted to the General Ledger account yet. This listing shows what will be posted after the next update.

The operator can specify whether or not the listing will include purchase order receipts.
