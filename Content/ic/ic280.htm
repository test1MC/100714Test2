Reorder Point Generation

 Last Revised: 01/07/14

Reorder Point Generation is used to create minimum restocking levels for items in Dynamo.

The operator must specify:

  1. the item sequence
  2. the item range
  3. the warehouse codes
  4. whether the application is run on only critical items or not
  5. the shelf supply in days
  6. the vendor lead time in days
  7. the safety stock in days
  8. the variation factor percent
  9. if warehouse records should be updated
  10. whether ABC values apply
  11. the minimum to maximum days
  12. whether or not unchanged items should print
  13. if months in which two times the average items were sold should be accounted for
  14. whether items lower than an operator-specified amount of periods should be excluded
  15. the weighting factor for each month

Based on these designations and other formulas, the application decides the best reorder point for all items specified.
