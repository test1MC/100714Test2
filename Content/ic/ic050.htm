Lead Time Maintenance & Listing

 Last Revised: 01/07/14

This program is used to create delay between the placement of an order and the delivery of the item(s) from the order.

Lead Time Maintenance & Listing Primary View
To Warehouse Choose the warehouse the item is being sent to.
Type Choose Vendor or Warehouse.
From Vendor/Warehouse Select the vendor or warehouse the item is being sent from.
Lead Time Type in the lead time in days.
C/D Type "C" to change the order or "D" to delete it.
