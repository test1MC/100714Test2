Inventory Requirements Report

 Last Revised: 08/30/13

Inventory Requirements Report is used to display a listing of non-rental items with Stock Status Quantities less than zero that appear on open sales orders. The application searches through all warehouses to find customer needs that have not yet been addressed and identifies what needs to be ordered from a vendor. The report displays the alpha name of the customer, the vendor number, the PCL, the item# and its description, and the needs for the quantity on hand, quantity to ship, quantity on order, quantity backordered, the stock status, the quantity ordered, the order date, the sales order number, and the vendor alpha name. This application can be run at any time.
