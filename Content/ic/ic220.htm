Warehouse Location Listing

 Last Revised: 02/06/14

Warehouse Location Listing is used to display a listing of where all items are located. The listing displays the item number and description, the product class, and the location number.

The operator must:

  1. choose the warehouse.
  2. indicate the item sequence.
  3. indicate the item range.
  4. specify whether or not the list should be printed in warehouse location order.
  5. indicate the format.
    1. All warehouse records
    2. Active items with no location
    3. Warehouse records with no location QOH
  6. enter the starting characters of the location code.
  7. specify whether or not MIN/MAX/QOH should be included.
