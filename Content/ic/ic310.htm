Record Inventory Usage

 Last Revised: 03/11/14

Record Inventory Usage allows the reporting of tax for non-sold items that were used within the your company. The application removes the item from inventory and posts the new information to the specified General Ledger account on the GLU menu, typically the Inventory Used in House account.

The operator must specify:

  1. The warehouse.
  2. The date the item was used.
  3. The items that were used within the company.
