Inventory Control

Change Average Cost

 Last Revised: 02/06/14

Change Average Cost is used to manually override the company-wide average cost for certain items. Though Dynamo generally determines the average cost itself, when a price has changed on an item, Dynamo does not recalculate the average cost. This application is only necessary for companies that use company-wide average costing.

 

The operator chooses the item and enters the new average cost.
