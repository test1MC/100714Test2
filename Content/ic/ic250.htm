Stock Status Reports

 Last Revised: 02/06/14

Stock Status Reports are used to give information about on-hand inventory. It displays all items in stock, all items understocked, and all items overstocked. It may also display future sales orders and future purchase orders. The stock status quantity, the minimum, and the maximum are also listed. The listing helps the operator to determine which items to buy and when to buy them based on their stock levels.

The operator must specify:

  1. the item sequence
  2. the range
  3. the warehouse(s)
  4. whether to include all items, active items, TDQ items, SSQ items, or MOH items
  5. whether or not to include the warehouse detail
  6. whether or not to print open order detail
