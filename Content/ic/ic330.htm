inventory control Utility Functions

Copy Warehouse Records

 Last Revised: 07/03/14

Copy Warehouse Records is used to duplicate item warehouse and minimum quantity records from one warehouse to another and will not overwrite existing records. This only applies to items with a positive minimum quantity. Enter the originating warehouse and the destination warehouse. Dynamo will display the progress and the amount of records processed as the program duplicates. This application may be used if a company has opened a new warehouse or moved another and the new location will have similar stock to the old warehouse.
