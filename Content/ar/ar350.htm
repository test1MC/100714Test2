A/R Listing by Invoice Number

 Last Revised: 07/19/13

The A/R Listing by Invoice Number lists, in order of invoice number, all of the accounts to which a company sells items or services. The list contains the invoice number, the customer number and name, the date, the type, the sales order number, the purchase order number/reference, the salesman, the warehouse, the branch, and the invoice amount.
