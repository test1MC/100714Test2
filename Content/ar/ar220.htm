A/R Statement Printing

 Last Revised: 02/04/14

A/R Statement Printing gives you the option to print Accounts Receivable Statements for specified customers.

The operator must indicate the receivables ageing date as well as whether to include the statement for selected customers or just one customer. The operator can select customers with a credit balance, with a future balance, and/or with a current balance. The operator can also indicate the amount of days past due necessary for an invoice to be included on the report. A message can also be included on the bottom of the report.

The statement of account can be given to the customer, and it includes the date, the invoice number, the order number, the type, the branch, the warehouse, the purchase order number/reference, the due date, and the amount of each invoice. The statement message will appear at the bottom of the statement.
