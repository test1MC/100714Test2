A/R Trial Balance

 Last Revised: 02/04/14

A/R Trial Balance is used to verify that debits and credits are equal. As available funds decrease, the credits inventory receives should increase. The amount should be balanced; i.e., it should be zero. If not, the application will display what customers owe. The invoice amounts will be displayed in groups of 30-day past-due date ranges. The printed report will display the customer number and name, the reference number, the sales order number, the date, the type, the branch, the warehouse, and the amount (positive or negative) of the transaction of each customer.
