Item Label Functions

Print Item Labels

 Last Revised: 02/06/14

Print Item Labels is used to define the dimensions and locations of each label on a page and then sends the information to be printed. UPC, EAN, and any other barcodes may be printed onto a label. This application requires a Zebra or Eltron printer to function. Contact Excellware, Inc. at (440) 866-6893 for help with designing your labels.
