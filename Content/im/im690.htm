Flexible Price List

 Last Revised: 01/07/14

Flexible Price List is used to print a list of pricing based on operator-designated specifications. It is flexible in that the operator can choose the specific items he/she wants on the listing; it is customizable.

The operator can type a two-lined title for the report. The operator must also indicate the effective date, whether or not to list customer specific pricing, the customer sequence/range, and the item sequence/range.

After completing those fields, you must specify the columns. Hit enter on any/all of the following pieces of information on the right side of the screen to include them on your listing:

The list can be customized based on the information a particular customer or salesperson might need to know.
