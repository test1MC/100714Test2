Sales by Item

 Last Revised: 01/29/14

Sales by Item is used to display sales by item lists sales (how many sold and how many times) that meet operator-specified criteria (vendor, PCL, item#, etc.) and period. The listing displays the profit margin for each of the items. The list displays the item number and description, the month to date units, month to date lines (how many times it has been a line item on a Sales Order this month), month to date sales, month to date cost, the year to date units, year to date lines (how many times it has been on a Sales Order this year), year to date sales, and year to date cost (all based on the specified date).

To view Item Search Options Invoked with F5, click here

Exclude Discontinued Items with No QOH Enable to exclude items that have no Quantity on Hand.
Exclude Items if Warehouse Qty Avail=0 Enable to exclude items unavailable in the warehouse.
Exclude Items if Company Qty Avail=0 Enable to exclude items unavailable at the company.
