Price Records Listing - by Item

 Last Revised: 02/06/14

Price Records Listing - by Item is used to display a list of price records by item number. The listing shows the item number and description, the customer, the date from and to, the type, and the factors.

To view Item Search Options Invoked with F5, click here

Exclude Discontinued Items with No QOH Enable to exclude items that have no Quantity on Hand.
Exclude Items if Warehouse Qty Avail=0 Enable to exclude items unavailable in the warehouse.
Exclude Items if Company Qty Avail=0 Enable to exclude items unavailable at the company.

The operator must enter:

  1. the start date
  2. the end date
  3. the item sequence
  4. the item range
  5. the page break
  6. whether to display standard pricing or customer exceptions

If customer exceptions is chosen, the operator must enter:

  1. the customer sequence
  2. the customer range
  3. the customer grouping
    1. each customer on a separate page
    2. show customer exceptions below the item
