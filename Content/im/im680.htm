Copy PCL Pricing Records

 Last Revised: 07/23/13

Copy PCL Pricing Records is used to copy a product-class specific pricing from one customer to another. The product class will then be applied to the new customer.

  1. Enter the product class to copy from.
  2. Enter the destination of the product class.
  3. Enter the customer record type.
    1. All Customers
    2. Standard Pricing Customer
    3. Customer Specific
  4. Enter the number of records.
  5. Enter the destination from date.
  6. Enter the destination to date.
