Customer Price & Discount Listing

 Last Revised: 02/06/14

Customer Price & Discount Listing displays a list of all the discounts listed for a particular customer. With some customers, you may have negotiated discount prices for specific items, for a certain vendor's products, or a specific category. The operator can view those discounted prices with this listing.

You can choose to view a single customer, a range of customers, or all customers at once. You can also choose to view by item# or specific product class.
