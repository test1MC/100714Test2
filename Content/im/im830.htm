Where Used Listing

 Last Revised: 10/04/13

The Where Used Listing is used to display in which assortments certain components are used. The list will show the component item number and description, its quantity, the description of its assortment, and that assortment's item number.

The operator specifies the sequence and the range for the listing.

To view Item Search Options Invoked with F5, click here

Exclude Discontinued Items with No QOH Enable to exclude items that have no Quantity on Hand.
Exclude Items if Warehouse Qty Avail=0 Enable to exclude items unavailable in the warehouse.
Exclude Items if Company Qty Avail=0 Enable to exclude items unavailable at the company.
