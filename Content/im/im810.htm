Assortment maintenance and listings

Compute Assortment Costs

 Last Revised: 07/24/13

Compute Assortment Costs is used to update the costs of the assortments in the item master file based on the components used in the assortments. If the prices of some of your items have changed, this application can be run to correct the cost of your assortments. The application checks the prices of each component item to compute the total cost of the assortment. The operator must pick the item sequence and range.
