Copy/Delete Price Exceptions by Item

Last Revised: 04/29/14

Copy/Delete Price Exceptions by Item is used to list customers with pricing records or to copy/delete pricing that is either customer-specific or standard. The application can be used to apply a percentage change to existing pricing.

To view Item Search Options Invoked with F5, click here

Exclude Discontinued Items with No QOH Enable to exclude items that have no Quantity on Hand.
Exclude Items if Warehouse Qty Avail=0 Enable to exclude items unavailable in the warehouse.
Exclude Items if Company Qty Avail=0 Enable to exclude items unavailable at the company.

 

The operator must specify:

  1. to list standard pricing, customer specific pricing, or customers with pricing records
  2. the item sequence
  3. the item range
  4. the existing from date
  5. the action (change factor by percentage or delete price exception records) 
  6. the percent change
  7. whether to apply it to the same customer or a different customer
  8. the from date
  9. the to date
