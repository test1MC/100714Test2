Cost and price maintenance and listings

Price List

 Last Revised: 02/06/14

Price List is used to display a price list effective after a specified date.

The operator must indicate the record format (customer or internal), the pricing exceptions, the pricing records format, the item sequence, the item range, the customer sequence, the customer range, and page breaks.

The resulting list displays the item number, any description(s), the quantity, and the selling price.

To view Item Search Options Invoked with F5, click here

Exclude Discontinued Items with No QOH Enable to exclude items that have no Quantity on Hand.
Exclude Items if Warehouse Qty Avail=0 Enable to exclude items unavailable in the warehouse.
Exclude Items if Company Qty Avail=0 Enable to exclude items unavailable at the company.
