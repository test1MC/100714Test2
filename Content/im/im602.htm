Standard Price List

 Last Revised: 02/06/14

Standard Price List displays the standard price for all items entered in Dynamo, effective from a date specified by the operator. The resulting list displays the item number, the description, the vendor part #, the UPC, the formula code (which calculates the selling price or cost), the cost, the cost unit of measure, the list price, the selling price, the list price gross margin percent, price exceptions, the quantity, the price calculation type, the selling price, and the gross margin percent. The operator enters the effective date, the item sequence, and the item range.
