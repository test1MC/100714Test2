Cost & price maintenance and listings

Net Price List

 Last Revised: 07/09/13

Net Price List is used to view pricing for particular items after all deductions, sales tax, etc. have been applied to the cost. However, the program will not show the formula used to determine the final net price of the item. The net price will simply be listed after the item number and its description.

To view Item Search Options Invoked with F5, click here

Exclude Discontinued Items with No QOH Enable to exclude items that have no Quantity on Hand.
Exclude Items if Warehouse Qty Avail=0 Enable to exclude items unavailable in the warehouse.
Exclude Items if Company Qty Avail=0 Enable to exclude items unavailable at the company.

Specify:

  1. the effective date,
  2. the customer specific pricing,
  3. the customer sequence,
  4. the customer range,
  5. the item sequence,
  6. the item range,
  7. whether or not the UPC code will show,
  8. whether or not the master quantity will show,
  9. whether or not the shelf quantity will show, whether or not the weight will show,
  10. whether or not the cost will show,
  11. whether or not the average cost will show, and
  12. what the selling price discount is.
