Price Records Listing - by Product Class

 Last Revised: 02/06/14

Price Records Listing - by Product Class is used to display a list of price records by product class. Along with the product class, the listing will display the description, the customer name and number, the date to and from, the type, and the factor.

The operator must specify the product class sequence and the product class range.
