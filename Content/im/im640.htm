Cost and price maintenance and listings

Copy/Delete Pricing Records

 Last Revised: 07/25/13

Copy/Delete Pricing Records is used to delete pricing records or change the cost or list prices by a certain percentage. It can be used in situations where a vendor does not supply a price list but, for example, has a 3% increase in price. The application will automatically calculate the new prices so the operator does not have to manually update them. The application can also be used to raise or lower prices for a range of items specified by the operator.

To view Item Search Options Invoked with F5, click here

Exclude Discontinued Items with No QOH Enable to exclude items that have no Quantity on Hand.
Exclude Items if Warehouse Qty Avail=0 Enable to exclude items unavailable in the warehouse.
Exclude Items if Company Qty Avail=0 Enable to exclude items unavailable at the company.

The operator must specify:

  1. the item sequence
  2. the item range
  3. the date type (records with specific from date or records with price effective date)
  4. the action (change cost & list prices by percentage or delete pricing records)
  5. the percent change for cost
  6. the percent change list
  7. new from date
  8. new to date
