General ledger reports

Standard Financial Statements

 Last Revised: 02/05/14

Dynamo General Ledger includes an Income Statement and Balance Sheet.

The operator specifies the Period Ending Date on which the reports are based.  Any date including dates in prior periods or fiscal years is permitted.

Comparison to the previous month or same month in prior fiscal year are available.

The Income Statement may be printed with Month and Year-to-Date information or Year-to-Date information only.

You may also consolidate multiple companies running the Dynamo General Ledger.  This option works best when the companies to be consolidated use the same Chart of Accounts, as the lines on the Financial Statements are accumulated by G/L Account#.
