General Ledger Postings

 Last Revised: 09/01/10

Dynamo is an integrated application.  This means that a transaction such as a sale, purchase, cash receipt, etc. makes a posting to the General Ledger.  The General Ledger account numbers used for these postings are specified and maintained in various places.
