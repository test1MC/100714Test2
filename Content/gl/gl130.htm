General Ledger

Chart of Accounts Listing

 Last Revised: 09/30/13

Your Chart of Accounts can be printed or displayed to the terminal using this option.

If you have established account groups (through Account Group Maintenance on the GLU menu), the group description will print on the Chart of Accounts Listing.

The listing will contain the group, the General Ledger Account Number, and the Description of the General Ledger Account Number.
