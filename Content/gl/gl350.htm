Inventory Used in House

 Last Revised: 02/05/14

This program allows the operator to view a listing of inventory used within his/her own company. The listing displays the warehouse, the general ledger date, whether or not it is on file, the cost, the unit, the General Ledger amount, the reference number, the login ID of the person who made the transaction, the batch number, the General Ledger debit, and the General Ledger credit. This report can be used to help prepare the use tax return, which needs to be paid on any items used in house (as they have not been sold).

Specify the desired starting and ending dates.
