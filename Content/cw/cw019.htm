Workday Calendar Maintenance

 Last Revised: 01/10/14

Use this program to maintain a workday calendar for your company.

Workday Calendar Maintenance View
Year Enter in the year you wish to maintain.
Saturdays Specify whether or not Saturdays are workdays.
Sundays Specify whether or not Sundays are workdays.
Month Enter in the month you would like to maintain. Touch enter to advance through the months.
Calendar Type the number of any additional dates (e.g., holidays) in which your company is closed.

 

Note: The asterisks indicate the dates in which your business is closed.
