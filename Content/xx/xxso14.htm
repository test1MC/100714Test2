Sales Order Processing Utility Functions

Sales Tax Control Maintenance

 Last Revised: 01/28/14

This application is used to manage all settings related to sales tax calculation.

Features:

  1. Single rate applied to entire state
  2. Multiple rates in state based on type of product, i.e., Illinois has different rates for sales, rental, and repairs
  3. Destination based or single rate for Ohio
  4. Date based rates so new rates can be entered in advance
  5. Option to charge sales tax on shipping (Ohio started the practice in August 2003)
  6. Branch specific tax calculation, i.e., internet sales branch using single state-wide rate, where branch in state uses product based rates
  7. Automatic updating of Ohio destination based sales tax rates.  Do not enter any Ohio county based rates.
  8. Support for 4 counties in central Ohio, Delaware, Fairfied, Licking, & Union counties, where part of the county is charged one tax rate, and the part of the county serviced by the Central Ohio Transit Authority (COTA) is charged another rate.
  9. G/L Account# for sales tax liability can vary by state, type of product, etc.
  10. Sales Tax report that includes Ohio's State Identifier numbers and not the Federal Information Processing Standard (FIPS).  For example the FIPS number for Cuyahoga County is 35 but Ohio uses a different State Identifier# of 18.  This report makes it easier to enter your monthly sales tax figures online.
  11. Creation of a file in the format required by Ohio for monthly upload of sales tax data, reducing entry errors and time to process.

Note: Only enter states that you charge sales tax.  Any state not listed will not be charged tax.  Ohio destination rates do not need to be entered here.  They can be downloaded automatically every month to insure latest rates are always in place.

Note: When making any rate change, you must re-enter all branch records with the new effective date, even if there are no rate changes for other records for the same branch.
