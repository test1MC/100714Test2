Searching for Customers

 Last Revised: 04/13/11

Dynamo includes a Google like ability to search for Customers.   Just enter what you know, and Dynamo will provide a list of matching customers.

Here are some examples of what you can enter.

You Enter Note
Dynamo Customer# You can always enter the Dynamo Customer#, if you know it.  But when you don't know it, enter something you do know from the list below.

Since a numeric entry such as 44001 might be a customer# or a zip code, Dynamo will list a customer with a Dynamo Customer# of 44001 as well as any customers with a zip code of 44001.  When you know the customer#, you can enter a space after the customer# to bypass searching for matching zip codes, etc.  For example, to select customer# 44001, and not display a list at all, enter 44001, then touch the space bar, then touch the Enter key.
Company Name Enter a few characters of the customer's 'Alphabetize by' field.  Matching customers will be included in the list regardless of whether the entry is made in upper, lower, or mixed case.
Contact Name You can enter a contact first name, i.e., John, their last name, i.e., Smith, or both names, i.e., John Smith.  You can enter the contact name in upper, lower, or mixed case.  
Email Address You can enter a contact's email address.  You can enter the email address in upper, lower, or mixed case.
Phone# You can enter the company telephone number, or any contact's telephone number, with or without the area code.  Since some area codes have changed, Dynamo will include in the list of matching customers, any phone# where the last 7 digits of the phone# match.  This feature helps you to find customers where the area code in the Dynamo records has not yet been changed.
Zip Code You may enter the starting characters of US zip codes.  For example entering 44001 will list all customers with a zip code of 44001, regardless of zip+4 value.  Entering 441, will include all customers with a zip code that begins with 441.
#+Invoice Number This option is most often used during Cash Receipts Entry when you know the invoice#, but not the Customer#.  Entering a pound sign, followed by the invoice number will return the customer# that was billed for the invoice number entered, i.e. an entry of #12345 will return the customer that was billed invoice# 12345.
F3=Help Select 'Item Level Help' to display this Help Text
F4=End Exit Item Search

Since searching for customers can sometimes take a while, especially when many items match the entry, you can also touch the F4 key during the search process to abort the search process, and display the customers found so far.

When multiple customers match the information entered, you will see a display similar to one of the examples below.  In some cases, you will need to touch the ArrowRight key to view additional columns.

Use the cursor movement keys to highlight the desired customer, then touch Enter.  The cursor movement keys include: ArrowDown, ArrowUp, Home, End, PageUp and PageDown.
