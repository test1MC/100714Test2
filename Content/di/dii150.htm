Inquiry Functions

Serialized Purchase History Inquiry

 Last Revised: 02/18/15

Serialized Purchase History Inquiry allows the operator to search for orders containing serialized items.

 

The operator must enter the first few characters of a serial number from one of the items on the order.

 

Items beginning with the entered characters will display in a list. If the operator selects one of these, the serial number can be changed if an error was made.
