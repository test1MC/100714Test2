Customer Maintenance Utility Functions

Change Customer# Listing

 Last Revised: 07/16/13

Change Customer# Listing is used to display a list of customers whose customer numbers have been changed in Dynamo. The list will show the name of the customer, the contact information about the customer, the old number, and the new number.
