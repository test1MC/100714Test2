Cash Receipts Listing & Deposit Ticket

 Last Revised: 02/05/14

Cash Receipts Listing & Deposit is used to display a listing of all cash receipts that were entered into Cash Receipts Entry and to print a deposit ticket for the checks. The application has recorded the information from the Cash Receipts entry and therefore will display the amount of checks and the deposit amount. The operator must choose the format (Deposit Ticket Only, Check Listing Only, or Both Check Listing & Deposit Ticket) and the deposit date.

The application will prompt you to insert plain paper for the listing and a deposit form for the deposit ticket. Please view our deposit ticket guidelines that describe which deposit tickets are compatible with Dynamo.

The listing will display the customer number, the date, the check number, and the check amount. The deposit ticket will display the amounts of all checks and give a check summary at the bottom.

Upon completion of this application, the operator can utilize Cash Receipts Application.
