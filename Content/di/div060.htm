A/R Ageing Detail, Summary, and Transaction Salesman

 Last Revised: 08/30/13

A/R Ageing Detail, Summary, and Transaction Salesman applications are used to display the money owed to you by customers as well as display how aged the debt is. The information is listed in 30 day increments.

Formats
A/R Ageing Detail Displays each customer and their debt information in detail, including finance charges of each invoice. Sample.
A/R Ageing Summary Displays ageing information by customer; the total for each appears on the report. Sample.
A/R Ageing by Transaction Salesman Displays information about the transactions completed by each salesperson. This report can be useful if a customer used several different salespeople within the operator's company and one of the salespeople needs to remind the customer to make payments.
