Print Envelope

 Last Revised: 10/15/13

Print Envelope creates and prints an envelope with customer, vendor, prospect, or salesperson address information included on it.

The operator can search for a customer, vendor, prospect, or salesperson by choosing one of those options from the main menu in this application. The operator can then search for one by typing in their number or contact name. The rest of the information will automatically fill in per your addresses created in Dynamo. At that point, you can, if desired, select a specific recipient from that company who the mail will go to.

By selecting Enter Address, the operator can enter in a new address.

By selecting Modify Printing Options, the operator can also specify if the envelope will be in uppercase letters and in which font the information will be printed.
