PO121

POP Assign PO#

 Last Revised: 06/29/07

This routine assigns a unique PO# to a new purchase order.

Purchase Order numbers are assigned sequentially from 1000 to 9999999.  The last purchase order number assigned is stored in the XXPO00 parameter file.  PO40$ is also written to the disc to insure the PO# is unique.

CALL "PO121", Y$, PO40$

PO121 Arguments
Field Passed To/From Description
Y$ To Session Control Variable
PO40$ To/From PO40.PONO$ is assigned a unique value.
