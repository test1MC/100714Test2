PO Receiving Enhancement Project: Summer 2008

 Last Revised: 02/19/09
Objectives
1 Enable a single vendor invoice to link to multiple PO Receipts
2 Enable an operator receiving a purchase order using a workstation, to exit the receipt, and continue later without having to re-enter all quantities received
3 Provide PO receipt timestamps
4 Provide ability to access PO receipts that have been updated to view heading, detail, timestamp, re-print any of the PO Receipt Listings.
5 Provide ability to re-open a receipt, change the date received, change or add new line items received, and update changes.
6 Display value of receipt on Receipt Heading display
7 Provide option to display value of receipt by PO
8 Provide a PO Receipt Listing that included value received by PO# and line item
9 Provide a display of Invoices linked to a PO Receipt including balance to be reconciled.
10 Provide a display of PO Receipts linked to a vendor invoice
11 Provide a method to maintain the vendor invoices linked to a PO receipt even when the invoice was entered and paid prior to the receipt
12 Provide a PO Receipt listing by Skid#, including the ability for the same item to appear on multiple skids.
13 Provide a method of entering PO receipts using a bar code scanner attached to a workstation, either by wire or wireless.
14 Eliminate the need to enter the Vendor# during PO receipt entry
15 Include a 'cartons' quantity on the PO Receipt Listing by Skid#
16 Provide a way to display the value of a PO receipt line item, even when this value differs from the value of the inventory received due to rounding issues created either by a PO discount% or multiplier.
17 Provide a way to permit to the cost on a PO, after a receipt has been started, but before it has been updated, and for these changes to be immediately reflected in the value of the received line item.
18 Provide a way to add line items to a PO, after a receipt has been started, and be able to receive these items on the existing PO receipt.
19 Provide a method to add a PO to an existing PO receipt, after receiving has been started.
20 Provide a menu selection that allows printing of all PO Receipt Listing options for operators not authorized to use the PO Receiving application.
21 Provide a place to store the ASN Quantity shipped as reported by vendor and include on variance listing when used.
22 Provide an automated method to install this enhancement and perform data conversion, with minimal manual tasks.

Installation Procedure
1 Install SMS update dated 7/31/08 or later if required
2 Copy from DS, Data Dictionary PO53 & PO54 files.
3 Delete & Copy from DS, Data Dictionaries for: PO50, PO51, PO52, AP13, IC03, IC10, IC020, IC030, and PO00.  Do not convert files to match dictionary.
4 Run Program PO168 and enter the company code to be converted.
5 Either copy menu POP from DS to XX, or use Menu Maintenance to copy PO Receiving from menu DS to XX.
6 Use Application Menu Maintenance (CWP) to copy PO300 menu from DS.
   
8 Export DY/PO
