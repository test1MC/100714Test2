AP101

Compute Invoice Amount allocated to PO Receipts

 Last Revised: 04/18/08

This routine is used to compute amounts allocated to PO Receipts for a given A/P Invoice.

It is used in Vendor Inquiry in the Accounts Payable Invoices option.

CALL "AP101", Y$, AP12$, AP101$

AP101 Arguments
Field Passed To/From Description
Y$ To Session Control Variable
AP12$ To A/P Vendor Invoice Heading record
AP101.NETINVOICE From Invoice amount less shipping charges
AP101.ALLOCATED From Invoice amount that has been allocated to a PO Receipt
AP101.BALANCE From Invoice amount to be allocated (or over allocated if negative)
