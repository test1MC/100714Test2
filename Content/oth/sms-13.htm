BBx Calculator

 Last Revised: 07/16/10

This calculator can be used like a regular desktop calculator with the added feature that the result of your calculations can be pasted into the current data entry field.  Though you can access the BBx calculator from the SMS menu, by default, the BBx calculator can be also accessed by touching the Hotkey (F1), then X.

To use the calculator to paste a calculation into a Dynamo numeric field, first advance the application to the numeric field, such as a price, that you want to compute.

Next Touch the Hotkey (F1), then X to launch the calculator.

Enter the numbers and operations desired.

Touch F10 or W to write (paste) the result into the active Dynamo field.
