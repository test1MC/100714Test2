Reprint Cash Drawer Payment Listing

 Last Revised: 08/20/13

Reprint Cash Drawer Payment Listing is used to print the payment listing from a specified cash drawer. The payment listing shows the payment type, the date of payment, the sales order number, the invoice number, the customer, and the payment.

To print, choose a branch, a cash drawer, and the batch number.
