Sales/Profit Analysis Reports

 Last Revised: 04/18/14

With Sales/Profit Analysis Reports, the operator can analyze business performance based on customer, salesman, branch, warehouse, and/or company totals compared with month, year, or percent change. This application would typically be run at month's end. The reports can help your company decide which customers should be contacted, for instance, if they are purchasing less than they have been previously.

The range, detail (none, product category type, product category, or product class), and month & year must be specified for any of the types of reports.

 

Rank Options
1. None The sales are listed only in the order specified previously by the report type (Customer, Salesman, Branch, Warehouse, or Company Totals).
2. Month's Sales The sales are listed based on raw sales dollars from highest to lowest in the month specified.
3. % Change in Month's Sales The report displays which customers are buying more than last month and which customers are buying less than last month.
4. Year's Sales The sales are listed based on raw sales dollars from highest to lowest in the year specified. This report can be run for the current year even if it is not at year end.
5. % Change in Year's Sales

The report displays which customers are buying more than last year and which customers are buying less than last year.
