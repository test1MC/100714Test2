Preliminary Invoice Register

 Last Revised: 02/04/16

Preliminary Invoice Register is used when an operator's invoices are scheduled to be updated. Using this program will allow you to view your invoices before they are updated for the final register. You can search for any errors and correct them before proceeding to create the final Invoice Register.

 

To customize the Preliminary Invoice Register, you will be asked to

Specify if you want all branches or just one.  
Specify if you want all warehouses or just one.  
Specify the date through which you want invoices.  
Choose the General Ledger posting date.  
Choose the detail option: print all line items, print only line items with an error code, or print no line items.  
Indicate the minimum gross margin percentage.  

 

Common Preliminary Invoice Register Errors
M - Margin
  • Your margin may be significantly higher or lower on this invoice than the specified amount for the company.
    		•
    Q - Insufficient quantity on hand You do not have enough of a particular item to fulfill the customer's order.
    S - Ship date You may not have selected a ship date for this particular invoice.

    Note also that a key will appear at the bottom of the register to indicate the meaning of error codes.

     

    After correcting any errors that may have been presented by the Preliminary Invoice Register, you should run the program again until no more errors appear. Once you have done so, you can run an Invoice Update on them and complete a final Invoice Register.
