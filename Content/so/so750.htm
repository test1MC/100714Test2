Quantity Sold by Item & Customer

 Last Revised: 08/09/13

Quantity Sold by Item & Customer is used to create and view a listing of the amount of a specified item purchased by a particular customer. The item number, item description, product class, customer name & number, the city & state, the quantity, and the amount will display on the listing.

Specify:

  • the starting month & year
  • the ending month & year
  • the branch
  • the item selection (all or specific)
  • the customer (all or specific)
  • the sequence
