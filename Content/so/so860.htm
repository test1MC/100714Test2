Historical Invoice Register

 Last Revised: 02/23/16

Historical Invoice Register is used to print and view listings of previous invoices in various summary formats.

 

First the operator will be asked to select a date range for the register by indicating the starting month & year as well as the ending month & year.

Next, choose the format of the report as follows:

Format:
1. Separate page for each salesman

This option prints a separate page for each salesman entered into Dynamo. One use for this option is to give every salesperson a copy of their report for the specified date range.

2. Sales Tax Information

Choosing this option will print a list of sales tax information for each customer invoice entered into Dynamo. This information includes the amount of the invoice, how much of it was taxable, the tax rate, as well as the sales tax collected. After specifying the date range for the register, the operator will be prompted for the level of detail to include in the report.

Detail:
List Taxable Invoices (Y/N) Specify whether to include those invoices that are taxable in the output.
List Non-Taxable Invoices (Y/N) Specify whether to include those invoices that are non-taxable in the output.

Print Tax Summary (Y/N)

 Specify whether to include a tax summary a the end of the output.

 

After specifying the level of detail, the branches to include can be chosen before generating the register.

3. Accounts Receivable

Creates a listing of the total sales, the shipping information, and payments.

5. Ohio Commercial Activity Tax

Creates a listing of all transactions during the specified period so that gross receipts can be reported for the Ohio Commercial Activity Tax (CAT). For more information on Ohio CAT, please consult the Ohio Department of Taxation.

Choose the desired branch to print the listing.
