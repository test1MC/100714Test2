Invoice Register

 Last Revised: 02/17/16

This program allows the operator to print a listing of invoices selected from a batch. Upon entering the application, choose the printer to print to, the batch number you'd like printed (the most recent batch number is the default), and then choose if you'd like them printed in the order they were entered or alphabetically. The Invoice Register will then print. If you previously corrected all of the errors identified on the Preliminary Invoice Register, there should be no new errors.

 

This register can be saved for your records.
