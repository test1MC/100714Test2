Sales by Shipping Method & Warehouse

 Last Revised: 04/18/14

Sales by Shipping Method & Warehouse is used to print a listing of sales by their shipping method in each warehouse. The listing gives details based on invoiced orders based on their shipping method.

Type in the starting month & year and the ending month & year in order to create the listing.

All of the information on the listing is separated by warehouse. The listing contains the amount of items shipped by pickup, by a company truck, by UPS/FedEx, by commercial carrier, and by any other method. The total sales for each warehouse are also listed.
