Sales Tax Reporting

 Last Revised: 01/08/14

Sales Tax Reporting is used to print or upload information about a company's sales for Ohio sales tax purposes. The information displayed provides details necessary for the Ohio tax website.

You must indicate the starting month & year, the branch code, the taxpayer ID, and the vendor license number. If you want to upload this information directly to the Ohio Sales Tax reporting website, view the Ohio Upload File Name. Upon logging into the website, the operator can upload the information by locating the upload file name that was given in Dynamo. The information is formatted in the manner required by the state of Ohio, which allows for easy upload. Currently, this function is only supported for Ohio companies.

Formats
No Invoice Level Detail

This format displays a summary of the sales tax.

Taxable Sales Only This format displays only sales that require tax, i.e. what is owed by the company. It begins with a summary and then continues in detail.
Non-Taxable Sales Only This format displays only tax-exempt sales. It can be used in instances of being audited.
All Sales

This format displays all of the above items.

The printed reports will display the taxing state, the state ID/county/tax authority code, the tax rate, the number of invoices, the gross sales, the exempt sales, the taxable sales, and the sales tax. They will also show the totals of all of these items.

To upload the file to the Ohio sales tax reporting website:

  1. Go to https://ohiobusinessgateway.ohio.gov/
  2. Login with your operatorname and password.
  3. Choose File/Pay Sales Tax Return.
  4. Choose Full UST1 file upload.
  5. Choose the correct file by clicking "Browse" and locate the sales tax report on your computer.
  6. Upload the file.
  7. Review the report for any errors.
  8. Accept the data.
  9. Choose the method of payment.
  10. Print the tax return for your own records.
