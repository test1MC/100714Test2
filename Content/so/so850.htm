Salesperson Invoice Register

 Last Revised: 08/09/13

Salesperson Invoice Register is used to create a list of all the invoices generated by a particular salesperson. The list will include the customer number and name, the invoice date, the invoice number, the sales order number, the sales, the cost, and the gross margin percent for each salesperson's sales.

To print the register, specify:

  • the start month/year
  • the ending month/year
  • the salesperson to be included on the list
