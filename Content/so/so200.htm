Open Sales Order Listing

 Last Revised: 08/22/13

This application prints a listing of any open sales orders based on a specified branch.

  1. Choose the desired branch.
  2. Choose from one of the following options to include on the listing:
    1. All open orders
    2. Open orders not yet invoiced
    3. Orders with payments not yet invoiced
    4. Orders marked as in use
  3. Choose Summary or Detail as the format.

 

The summary will display the order date, the sales order number, the customer number & name, the ship date, the invoice number, the invoice date, the order value, and the sales.

The detailed list will display items (number and description) listed by customer, and the customer is then listed by order date in addition to the shipping dates, the invoice numbers, the invoice date, the order value, the sales, the quantity ordered, and the net price. It also shows the final total.
