Print Unused Sales Order Numbers

 Last Revised: 07/19/13

This application is used to print numbers that have not been used for any sales orders based on the numbers assigned in Assign/Delete Sales Order Numbers.

Choose the desired printer, and then choose the last unused number you want included on your list.

Once you have done so, unused sales order numbers will print. These are the numbers you have available for any future sales orders.
