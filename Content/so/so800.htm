Change in Sales/Margin by Customer

 Last Revised: 08/30/13

Change in Sales/Margin by Customer is used to view a listing of the change in sales or margin by customer. It displays the customer name & number, the salesperson, the selected year's sales/margin, a prediction of the upcoming year's sales/margin, and the change in sales/margin. The report can be used to examine which customers are buying from your company more or less than the previous year.

The operator must specify:

  • the month and the year
  • the amount of customers to rank
  • to include sales or margin
  • costed, uncosted, or all

Note: If you choose Margin, costed is the only option.
