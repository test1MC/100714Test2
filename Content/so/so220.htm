New Order Fill Rate Report

 Last Revised: 07/11/13

New Order Fill Rate Report is used to display how many sales orders have been created completely out of inventory, i.e. how many line items were not backorders. The report displays these figures based on the warehouse, the location, the month, and the year.

To begin, type or select the batch number (the most recent one is the default), and the date will automatically fill in.
